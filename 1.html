Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha is a character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. Itachi is the older brother of Sasuke Uchiha, and is responsible for killing all the members of their clan, sparing only Sasuke.
Clan: Uchiha
Powers and abilities: Pyrokinesis, Illusion, Power Mimicry, Superhuman speed
Played by: Hideo Ishikawa
Akatsuki partner: Kisame Hoshigaki (Rogue ninja)
Created by: Masashi Kishimoto
First appearance: Naruto chapter #139;
Naruto episode #81: Return of the morning mist
Relatives: Sasuke Uchiha (younger brother); Sakura Haruno (sister-in-law); Sarada Uchiha (niece)
